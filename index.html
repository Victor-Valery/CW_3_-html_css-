The Float Properties

The float property is used for positioning and formatting content. The float property can have one of the following values:
left: The element floats to the left of its container.
right: The element floats to the right of its container.
none: Default. The element does not float.
inherit: The element inherits the float value of its parent.



Opacity, Transparency


The `opacity` property specifies the opacity/transparency of an element.
The `opacity` can take value from 0 to 1. The lower value, the more transparent.
